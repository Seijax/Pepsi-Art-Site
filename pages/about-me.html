Hello World

My name is Petya Kostova. I love people and music, love to dance, to create, to draw, to code, to get out of my comfort zone and to improve myself. My mission is to inspiring people to be the best version of themselves, to develop more tolerance and love for themselves and others. The sense of helping people to feel better, to overcome the circumstances, to follow their dreams, is inestimable to me. There is something magical feeling the joy in their eyes and heart.

My personal interests are:

  • Software Development and New Technologies;
  • Web, Graphic, UX, UI, Conceptual, Product, Interior and Fashion Design;
  • Art (Painting) and Digital art;
  • Personal Development and Psychology;
  • Blogging and Marketing;
  • Music and Dances.

I created this page to share my artworks with You. Here You can find my designs, paintings, drawings and projects.
